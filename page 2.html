The Philippines is widely famous for its amazing and beautiful tourist spots. People from all around the world come to visit the Philippines just to see its beautiful beaches, mountains and historical architectures. So without further a do, here are the top three tourist spots most visited in the Philippines.







El Nido Palawan

El Nido Palawan: Famous for its white-sand beaches, coral reefs and clear turquoise waters.

Chocolate hills

The Chocolate hills, established in 1988, is a popular tourist spot for its beautiful chocolate looking perfect hills that is located on the island of bohol.

Mt. Pinatubo

Mt. pinatubo is a very famous volcano in the philippines that alot of tourist come to visit, its located in the Zambales Mountains and it is aswell an active stratovolcano.
